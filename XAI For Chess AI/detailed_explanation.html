Detailed Explanations

Feature Meanings

Material Advantage: The value of the pieces on the board, considering captures and losses.

Center Control: Control over the central squares of the board, which is crucial for overall board dominance.

King Safety: The safety of the king, including factors like castling and piece placement around the king.

Mobility: The number of valid moves available, reflecting the flexibility and potential of pieces.

Attack Coverage: The number of squares controlled or attacked, putting pressure on the opponent's pieces.

Defense Stance: The defensive positioning of pieces, protecting against threats and securing key positions.
